MPQ Planner

Contribute on Github

This is a tool to help you plan your Marvel Puzzle Quest Party. Pick a character and you will be able to see what active color abilities they bring to your team, and what passives. Using our graph you can pick out complementary characters that will help you succeed no matter what tiles are on the board.
